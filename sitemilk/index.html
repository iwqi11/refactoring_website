Bicycle Gear Design

Bestcogs.com is your one-stop site for designing bicycle gearing systems. We aim to offer a simple, flexible interface that will let you design an optimal gear setup for your bike.

Get Started

If you just want to design a gearing system, click here and choose your chainwheels, cassette/freewheel parameters, and gearing preferences. Your ideal freewheel options will be calculated for you.

Or perhaps you want more detailed instructions.That's often the best way to approach things (RTFM, as they say).

Other Information

Cycling Tips

When choosing your bike gears, You need to assess your riding style, think about the terrain you'll be riding on and consult with local bike shops for expert advice.
